F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Which data sources can Trinetro Labs connect to?

The platform connects to 50+ Indian and global tools including Shopify, Tally, Razorpay, Google Sheets, CRMs, data warehouses, and APIs — all unified into one analytics layer.

Is Trinetro Labs secure for enterprise use?

Yes. The platform is built with enterprise-grade security, access controls, and compliance standards to protect sensitive business data across teams and departments.

Which industries benefit most from Trinetro Labs?

E-commerce, BFSI, retail, healthcare, logistics, and fast-growing mid-sized companies benefit the most by reducing reporting delays and improving operational intelligence.

How can my company get started with Trinetro Labs?

You can request a demo, connect your existing tools in minutes, and start exploring real-time insights immediately with guided onboarding support.

Do I need technical or SQL knowledge to use Trinetro Labs?

No. Trinetro Labs is built for business users. You can ask questions in plain English and instantly receive dashboards and insights, while analysts still have advanced scripting options if needed.
